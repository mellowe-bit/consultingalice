About Alice

HR leadership that feels like a partnership, not a policy manual

With over 9 years of progressive HR experience, I've led people strategy at organizations ranging from high-growth startups to enterprise operations. My career includes impactful roles at Amazon, Safelite AutoGlass, and Express Employment Professionals, where I honed my ability to balance business objectives with genuine care for employees.

I founded Consulting Alice to bring that same strategic, people-first approach to growing businesses that need HR leadership or a strategic business/workforce partner without the full-time overhead. Whether you're navigating complex compliance challenges, building performance frameworks, or transforming your company culture, I bring warmth, clarity, and real-world expertise to every engagement.

What I Do

Strategic HR services tailored to your business

From foundational compliance to forward-thinking people strategy, every engagement is customized to where you are — and where you want to go.

Strategic HR Planning

Align your people strategy with business goals through workforce planning, organizational design, and data-driven decision making.

AI & Systems Integration

Leverage AI tools and modern HR systems to automate workflows, improve analytics, and make smarter people decisions faster.

Policies & Handbooks

Create clear, compliant, and human-friendly employee handbooks and HR policies tailored to your culture and jurisdiction.

Performance Management

Design review cycles, goal frameworks, and feedback systems that drive accountability and growth — not just annual check-the-box exercises.

Employee Relations

Navigate sensitive workplace situations with confidence — investigations, conflict resolution, and culture-building that protects your people and your business.

Compliance & Risk

Stay ahead of federal, state, and local employment regulations with proactive audits, risk assessments, and ongoing compliance support.

Leave & Accommodations

Manage FMLA, ADA, and state-specific leave programs with clarity and compassion, ensuring compliance while supporting your employees.

Talent Management

Attract, develop, and retain top talent with strategic recruiting, onboarding programs, succession planning, and career pathways.

HR Tech Implementation

Select, implement, and optimize HRIS platforms and people tools that streamline operations and provide actionable workforce insights.

Workforce Hiring Strategies

Develop targeted recruitment approaches that attract the right talent — from sourcing pipelines and employer branding to interview frameworks and offer strategies.

Leadership Development & Coaching

Grow confident, capable leaders at every level through executive coaching, management training, and customized development programs that drive results.

Change Management

Guide your organization through transitions with structured change frameworks, stakeholder alignment, communication plans, and adoption strategies that minimize disruption.
